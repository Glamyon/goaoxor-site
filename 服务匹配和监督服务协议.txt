2️⃣ 自动计算逻辑
	1.	启动费固定 $30（第一档项目总额 ≤ $300 也是 $30）
	2.	中介费按项目总额计算剩余费用：
	•	$0–$300 → 已支付启动费 $30，协议完成
	•	$301–$800 → 中介费 = 10% × 项目总额 – $30
	•	$801–$2000 → 8% × 项目总额 – $30
	•	$2001–$5000 → 6% × 项目总额 – $30
	•	$5001+ → 5% × 项目总额 – $30

生成 PDF 时自动填入“剩余中介费”。

服务匹配与监督协议

1. Parties / 签署方

Client / 客户: ________________________
Goaoxor (Intermediation Platform / 基于 Goaoxor.com 的中介平台): Goaoxor

2. Governing Law / 司法管辖

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Hong Kong.
本协议适用香港法律，并按其解释。

3. Scope of Services / 服务范围

Goaoxor shall:
	1.	Match the Client with suitable Project Production Parties based on the Client’s project requirements.
根据客户项目需求，将客户与合适的项目制作方进行匹配。
	2.	Provide supervision services to ensure the agreed project milestones are communicated and tracked.
提供监督服务，确保项目各阶段按约沟通并跟进。
	3.	Not engage in project execution funds or manage the payment between Client and Project Production Party.
不参与项目执行款或管理客户与项目制作方之间的支付。

4. Client Obligations / 客户义务
	•	Provide accurate project requirements, expected deliverables, and budget information.
提供准确的项目需求、预期成果及预算信息。
	•	Respond timely to communications from Goaoxor and Project Production Party.
及时回复来自Goaoxor及项目制作方的沟通。
	•	Pay the Intermediation Fee (including supervision services) according to this Agreement.
按本协议规定支付中介服务费（含监督服务）。

5. Intermediation Fee / 中介服务费（含代监督服务）
Project Value / 项目总额
Intermediation Fee / 中介服务费
$0 – $300
$30 (fixed) / 固定30美元
$301 – $800
10% of project total / 项目总额的10%
$801 – $2,000
8% of project total / 项目总额的8%
$2,001 – $5,000
6% of project total / 项目总额的6%
$5,001+
5% of project total / 项目总额的5%

Payment Terms / 支付条款:
	1.	Start-up Fee / 启动费: $30 payable upon signing this Agreement.
首付启动费：签署协议时支付30美元。
	2.	Remaining Intermediation Fee / 剩余中介费: After Client and Project Production Party agree on the project total, Client shall pay the remaining fee calculated according to the table above minus the $30 already paid.
剩余中介服务费：客户与项目制作方确认项目总额后，按上表百分比计算中介费，并减去已支付的$30。
	3.	Payment Method / 支付方式: Via Wise or other agreed international transfer.
支付方式：通过Wise或其他双方约定的国际转账方式。
	4.	Non-refundable / 不可退款: All paid fees are non-refundable.
已支付费用不可退还。

6. Limitation of Liability / 责任限制

Goaoxor shall exercise reasonable care in providing intermediation and supervision services.
Goaoxor 在提供匹配和监督服务时将尽合理注意义务。

However, Goaoxor shall not be liable for any direct, indirect, or consequential loss, delay, or failure arising from the performance or non-performance of the Project Production Party.
但对于项目制作方履约或未履约导致的任何直接、间接或连带损失、延误或失败，Goaoxor 不承担责任。

7. Confidentiality / 保密

Both parties shall maintain the confidentiality of any information obtained during the course of this Agreement.
双方须对在协议期间获知的所有信息保密。

8. Dispute Resolution / 争议解决
	•	Parties shall first attempt to resolve disputes through friendly negotiation.
双方应首先通过友好协商解决争议。
	•	Failing negotiation, Hong Kong courts shall have exclusive jurisdiction.
协商不成时，香港法院享有专属管辖权。

Note: The Intermediation Fee includes supervision services. The Agreement becomes effective upon Client’s signature. Goaoxor, as the fixed signing party, does not require repeated signatures.
